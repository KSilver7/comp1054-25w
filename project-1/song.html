The Song

Magenta is a song that can mean different things for many people. I like that it's name is very memorable. It tackles feelings of loneliness and isolation, frustration and sadness, and the loss of hope, while providing words of encouragement and compantionship that people need to hear when they are feeling these things and hitting their low points. It's a song that's been there at the right time when it's been needed. Plus, the music itself is very upbeat.

Nano

Nano's real name is Hana Ishiyama Ohara. She was born in the USA in 1988. However, she moved to Japan in her teens.

She is bilingual and can sing in English and Japenese. She creates cover songs for anime and Vocaloid songs. She also sings original songs for various anime and video games.

She sings, writes her own lyrics, and can play the guitar.

Devilish-P

Devilish-P, who also goes by the name of Darvish-P and devilish5150 is a Vocaloid producer and composer, as well as an illustrator on various platforms including Youtube. His age is unclear.

He plays the guitar, often doing solos for himself and others.
